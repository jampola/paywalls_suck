About this abomination

I was on a new laptop with no internet in the middle of nowhere, but I remember I had a small collection of Python libs sitting in some other projects, so I toyed with the idea of creating something solely based on what I had available. A few beers and as many hours later, this is it!

How long are the images stored?
How long images remain on the server depends on the traffic. This is intended to be a service for rendering images, not hosting them. In saying that, I'm not going to lose sleep if someone sends the link to a few people, however I don't particularly wish to get /.ed or a Reddit hug of death!
tldr: Cron will delete them after a predetermined period of time.
